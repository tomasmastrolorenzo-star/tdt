# SEO Strategy: Organic Growth Engine

## 🎯 Objective
Rank for **high-intent transactional keywords** to drive free traffic.

## 1. 🔑 Keyword Strategy (The "Money" Keywords)

### Primary Keywords (High Difficulty, High Volume)
- "Buy Instagram Followers"
- "Buy TikTok Likes"
- "Comprar Seguidores Instagram Argentina" (Local SEO)

### Long-Tail Keywords (Low Difficulty, High Conversion)
- "Best site to buy real instagram followers 2025"
- "Cheap tiktok views instant delivery"
- "Como crecer en instagram rapido" (Blog content)

## 2. 🏗️ Technical SEO Checklist
- **Meta Titles:** Format: `[Service] | [Benefit] | Trend Up`
  - *Ex:* "Buy Instagram Followers | Instant & Real | Trend Up"
- **Meta Descriptions:** CTR focused. "Get 1k followers in 5 minutes. 100% Safe. No Password Required."
- **Sitemap:** Auto-generated `sitemap.xml` for all service pages.
- **Schema Markup:** `Product` schema for service pages (shows price/rating in Google).
- **Speed:** Core Web Vitals optimization (Next.js Image, dynamic imports).

## 3. 📝 Content Pillars (Blog)
Create a `/blog` section to capture informational traffic.
1.  **Tutorials:** "How to go viral on TikTok in 2025"
2.  **Comparisons:** "Buying Followers vs. Ads: What works better?"
3.  **Case Studies:** "How this account gained 10k followers in a week"

## 4. 🔗 Backlink Strategy
- **Guest Posting:** Write for marketing blogs.
- **Directories:** List Trend Up in SaaS/Tool directories.
- **Social Signals:** Active Pinterest/Twitter accounts linking back.
