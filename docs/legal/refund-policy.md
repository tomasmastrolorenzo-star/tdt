# Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** November 28, 2025

## 🛡️ Our Guarantee
We stand behind our services. If we fail to deliver your order, you are entitled to a refund.

## 1. Eligibility for Refund
- **Non-Delivery:** If the service has not started within 72 hours of purchase.
- **Product Not as Described:** If the delivered service differs significantly from the description.

## 2. Non-Refundable Cases
- **Private Accounts:** If your account is set to "Private" during delivery, the system will fail, and **no refund** will be issued.
- **Wrong Link:** If you provide an incorrect URL (e.g., wrong username), we cannot reverse the order.
- **Change of Mind:** We do not offer refunds if you simply change your mind after the order is processed.

## 3. Refill Policy (Warranty)
- For "Refill Guaranteed" services (e.g., 30-Day Refill), if your follower count drops below the purchased amount, we will refill it for free within the warranty period.
- To request a refill, contact support with your Order ID.

## 4. How to Request a Refund
Contact us at `support@trendup.com` with:
1. Order ID
2. Reason for refund
3. Screenshot of the issue (if applicable)

We aim to resolve all disputes within 24 hours.

---
*Note: Filing a chargeback without contacting us first will result in a permanent ban from our platform.*
